Who I Am

About Me

I'm a QA Engineer with experience across diverse domains — from large-scale SaaS marketplaces and agro-tech platforms to e-commerce websites, mobile apps, and school management systems. I've been involved throughout the full QA lifecycle: writing and executing test cases, performing manual and automated testing, reporting bugs, validating fixes, and supporting release sign-offs.

I've also worked closely with clients and cross-functional teams by conducting meetings, presenting product updates, reviewing support tickets, and translating client feedback into clear and actionable Jira tasks for developers. This experience helped me build strong communication skills and bridge the gap between business requirements and technical implementation.

Beyond manual testing, I've developed and maintained Python Selenium automation scripts on live projects for regression testing, UI validations, and repetitive workflows. I enjoy building efficient QA processes that improve reliability, reduce manual effort, and make releases smoother and more consistent.
