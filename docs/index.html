About Me

I'm a QA Engineer with experience across diverse domains from large-scale SaaS marketplaces and agro-tech platforms to e-commerce sites, mobile apps, and school management systems. I've been involved in the full QA lifecycle: writing test cases, running manual and automated tests, filing bugs, and signing off on releases.

I've also worked closely with clients presenting product updates, conducting meetings, reviewing support tickets, and translating client feedback into actionable Jira issues for the dev team. I know how to communicate clearly on both sides of the table.

For automation and performance testing I use Python with Selenium. I focus on manual testing at my core, but I'm always pushing to do more smarter, faster, and with fewer surprises at launch.
